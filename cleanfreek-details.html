Clean FrEEK

The concept for Clean FrEEK was inspired by a Youtuber who cleans her subscribers homes for free, primarily for the ones who are going through hard times in their life. The goal of the app is to lighten the burden of people who may have a sizable mess that is overwhelming them.

Users can create an account as a customer or a cleaner. Once their profile is set up, they may create a cleaning request post or respond to one within their area. Cleaning requests may be accepted, rejected or negotiated in regards to price and job time frame. Once a job is accepted, the time scheduled will be added to an in-app calendar with payments and invoices/receipts being processed within the app. Customers can leave reviews for cleaners and vice versa.
